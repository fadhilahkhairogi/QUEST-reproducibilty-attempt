Candidate Sentence: Wesleyan College The Basics About Wesleyan College Wesleyan College is a private institution that was founded in 1836. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 624, its setting is suburban, and the campus size is 200 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar.  Wesleyan College's ranking in the 2011 edition of Best Colleges is National Liberal Arts Colleges, 158. Its tuition and fees are $18,000.

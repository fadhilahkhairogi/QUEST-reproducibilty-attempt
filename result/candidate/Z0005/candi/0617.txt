Candidate Sentence: Towson University The Basics About Towson University Towson University is a public institution that was founded in 1866. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 17,148, its setting is suburban, and the campus size is 328 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar.  Towson University's ranking in the 2011 edition of Best Colleges is Regional Universities (North), 46. Its in-state tuition and fees are $7,656; out-of-state tuition and fees are $19,114.
